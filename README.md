🇮🇳 India Tourism & Footfall Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes tourism trends, visitor footfall, domestic and foreign tourist arrivals, revenue generation, and regional tourism performance across India.

📌 Project Overview

The India Tourism & Footfall Analysis Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI project designed to analyze tourism patterns across India. It provides valuable insights into visitor footfall, domestic and international tourist arrivals, state-wise tourism performance, revenue contribution, seasonal trends, and popular tourist destinations.

Using interactive visualizations and business intelligence techniques, the dashboard enables stakeholders to identify tourism trends, evaluate destination performance, and support data-driven decision-making for the tourism industry.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze tourism trends across Indian states and union territories.
Compare domestic and foreign tourist arrivals.
Measure visitor footfall at major tourist destinations.
Identify seasonal tourism patterns.
Evaluate tourism revenue and economic contribution.
Discover high-performing tourist destinations using interactive Power BI visuals.

🔍 Key Insights
🇮🇳 Domestic Tourism
Domestic tourists contribute the largest share of India's tourism sector.
Festival seasons and public holidays significantly increase visitor footfall.
Heritage, religious, and hill stations attract the highest number of visitors.
🌍 International Tourism
Foreign tourist arrivals contribute substantially to tourism revenue.
Medical tourism, cultural tourism, and heritage tourism continue to attract global visitors.
Improved connectivity and digital services support international tourism growth.
📈 Tourism Revenue
Higher tourist arrivals directly increase hospitality, transportation, and local business revenues.
Tourism generates employment across hotels, restaurants, travel agencies, and retail sectors.
Well-developed tourist destinations consistently contribute more to the economy.
